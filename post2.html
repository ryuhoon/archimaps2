GUNDAM FACTORY YOKOHAMA

As you walk toward Yamashita Park in Yokohama, you will see a giant Gundam robot from the pier. The RX-78F00 has a 1:1 scale of gundam on the dock. The RX-78F00 seems to be a variant of the RX-78 gas, the first thing that comes to mind when you think of it.

It will be open until 31 March 2022. The Gundam Factory consists of one and two floors, with the RX-78F00 standing on the Gundam dock. I don't know if there's a set time, but Gundam sometimes moves. Gundam moves as voice actors narrate with a rash simulation. It also shows the movement of the joints, such as knee posture and arm lifting.

The first floor has an academy and a bass. The academy is a facility for robot technology, AR experience, etc. Base is selling Gundam-related goods. There is a conference room and a cafe on the second floor. Gundam characters such as bread and coffee packaging sold at cafes are depicted. Admission fees are different from those on the Gundam dock observatory. The entrance fee for academies and cafes is 1,650MW per adult. Up to the observatory is 3,300 MW for adults and children.

Gundam is very big, so you don't have to go inside. If you look closely, you feel that the details and things are very well done. Last year, in Odaiba, the actual size of the unicorn gundam stood. I don't know much about Gundam. From a mania's point of view, this kind of event is very good.
